ABOUT ME

I’m Amel, a sound designer, mixer, and dialogue editor based in Montréal. Audio post-production blends my love for multimedia with my passion for sound and music—it’s where I get to bring stories to life.

My work spans a wide range of formats, from TV series and documentaries to advertising and podcasts. I handle everything from sound design, mixing, and dialogue editing to Foley and studio recording. I also create closed captions and burn-in subtitles in French and English, making sure accessibility never comes at the cost of tone or impact.
